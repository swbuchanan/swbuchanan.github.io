My work

Right now I’m studying geometric flows under Mat Langford at ANU’s Mathematical Sciences Institute. I’m working on Ricci flow and a related conformal flow. Elsewhere on the site I have a brief technical description of some of what I’m working on at the moment.

If you are curious about geometric flows, I have started a series of posts introducing this topic to a general audience. See the first one here.

For an interactive demonstration of a geometric flow with some applications in materials science, see Anthony Carapetis’s page for a nice visualization of the curve shortening flow.

My earliest post also has a couple of similar things to play with.
